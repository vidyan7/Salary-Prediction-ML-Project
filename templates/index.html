Our Methodology

Step-by-step process for building accurate and transparent salary prediction models

Methodology

Problem Definition

We define the goal of salary prediction, outline the scope, and identify influencing factors like experience, skills, and location.

Data Acquisition

Salary datasets are collected from job portals, government reports, and anonymized user submissions to ensure diverse and reliable data.

Data Preprocessing

We clean, normalize, and validate the dataset, handling missing values and encoding categorical variables for accurate modeling.

Feature Engineering

We extract and transform key features such as education, skills, and industry to improve model accuracy and prediction power.

Model Development

We train machine learning models such as Linear Regression, Random Forest, and Neural Networks to capture salary trends effectively.

Model Evaluation

Models are evaluated using metrics like RMSE, MAE, and R¬≤ Score, with cross-validation ensuring robustness and fairness.

Deployment & Benchmarking

Validated models are deployed to our platform and benchmarked against industry salary standards for accurate predictions.

Privacy & Maintenance

We ensure strict data privacy and continuously update models with new market data to maintain reliability over time.
